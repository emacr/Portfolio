## Project Context

This application complements the WhatsApp order automation flow, providing a visual interface for restaurant staff to manage orders in real-time according to their role within the operation.

## Roles and Features

- **Kitchen**: Views incoming orders and marks preparation as complete.
- **Supervisor**: Monitors all order statuses and handles incidents.
- **Courier**: Sees orders ready for delivery with customer details.
